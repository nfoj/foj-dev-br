Problem:

Your task is to use for loops to display only odd natural numbers from 1 to 99.

# Output

  • 1
  • 3
  • 5
  • .
  • .
  • .
  • 99

# Solution

            
  for i in {1..99..2}; do 
      echo "$i"
  done

# Solution_01

            
  for ((i=1; i<=99; i=i+2)); do
      echo "$i"
  done

# Solution_02

            
  seq 1 2 99
