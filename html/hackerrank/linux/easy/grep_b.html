Problem:

N credit card numbers. Assume that the credit card numbers will have 4 space separated segments with 4 digits each.

# Input

  • 1234 5678 9101 1234
  • 2999 5178 9101 2234
  • 9999 5628 9201 1232
  • 8482 3678 9102 1232

# Output

  • 1234 5678 9101 1234
  • 2999 5178 9101 2234
  • 9999 5628 9201 1232

# Solution

            
  grep '\(\d\)\s*\1'
