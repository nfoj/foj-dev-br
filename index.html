Hello,

Welcome to our small and humble space.

As a courteous guest, please remove your shoes before entering. Remember, we all share this common space. As a polite visitor, please be mindful of your surroundings and keep the area tidy. If you've brought something to share, we welcome it. If not, please leave it outside.

Please make yourself comfortable in the chair, get a glass of water or coffee, and let's share something together.

This small project aims to share programming knowledge in a simple and accessible way, allowing us to have conversations even without prior experience.

The texts will be written and posted on the GitHub page in advance, allowing you to contribute by making improvements as the content is developed.
